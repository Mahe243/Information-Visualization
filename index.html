Exploring Stock Market Insights:

Unlocking the power of data, this project showcases Python's prowess in visualizing stock market trends. I had uncovered intricate relationships among indices through a comprehensive correlation matrix spanning a decade. Additionally, by spotlighting days when indices yielded over 2% returns to identify potential investment opportunities by Python's visualization libraries to illuminate market insights that aid investors in making informed decisions.

Experience the Stock Market Dynamics:

This Tableau dashboard offers a comprehensive view of key stock market indicators. From moving averages and open prices to a detailed price table of selected indices, this dashboard provides insights into price percent changes and the highest and lowest prices during the specified period here I had taken it as one year. With adjustable start and end dates and the flexibility to choose specific index names, this interactive dashboard is your gateway to understanding the intricate price trends that shape the market.

  • Tableau

Unveiling Market Connections with Gephi:

This visualization harnesses the power of Gephi to map the intricate web of connections between stock market indices, their constituent companies, and their respective industries. By graphically representing the relationships between index names, associated companies, and their industries, this visualization offers a unique perspective on the interplay of diverse market elements. Explore the dynamic network that underlies the stock market landscape, providing valuable insights into the conglomerate of industries that collectively shape its trajectory.
